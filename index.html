FAQ




Are beginners welcome?


Absolutely! I believe there's information in the course for people at all levels of photography. We will take a look the basics of Lightroom as well as my philosophy for composition and camera settings before working our way into in-depth technical editing processes.



Is the course Lightroom based?


This masterclass will focus specifically on Lightroom classic. We will go deep into every slider and mask in the develop module and how they can all work together for cohesive stylised edits. We will also be touching on a few additional techniques with Topaz Labs AI plugins and a section on Davinci Resolve for video reels.


What can we expect?


We'll be looking at my entire Adobe Lightroom workflow, from importing images and organising your shoot catalogs, to making selections, in depth masking processes and colour grading, then how I prepare and export images for use in print and social media.



Will I be able to rewatch the episodes in the future?


Yes! You will have lifetime access to the episodes, which will be available to watch on any device.



Does your editing course apply to other genres of photography?


We will be learning techniques that can be applied to any style of image. We'll also be looking at some of my commercial and portrait work for specific lessons on editing skin tones, products and how to incorporate brands into your trips.
